# **Comprehensive Strategic Financial Analysis and Multi-Year Operational Projections for the Transition of Podere Cerreto to Agriturismo Violiveto**

The transformation of Podere Cerreto into Agriturismo Violiveto represents a highly sophisticated integration of high-value agriculture and luxury hospitality. Central to this strategy is the optimization of a 24-hectare estate in Castel Viscardo, ensuring compliance with Umbrian Regional Law (L.R.) 1/2015 while maximizing revenue through a dual-entity structure.1 This model strategically separates standard agriturismo operations from high-turnover experiential services to maintain the critical 50% revenue ceiling required for fiscal benefits.4

## **Dual-Entity Corporate Architecture**

To maximize growth without jeopardizing the agriturismo tax status, the business is structured into two distinct legal entities:

1. **Agriturismo Violiveto (Agricultural Entity):** Focuses on saffron, olive oil, and wine production, along with overnight guest accommodations. This entity benefits from the *Regime Forfettario Agrituristico* (taxation on only 25% of gross revenue).1  
2. **Violiveto Experience & Events (Affiliate Service Entity):** A separate *Società a Responsabilità Limitata* (SRL) that manages high-margin services such as day-trip wine tours, weddings, and wellness day passes. This separation ensures that the income from these activities does not count toward the agriturismo’s hospitality revenue cap.

## **Implementation Strategy: Entity Phasing**

* **Q3 2026:** Finalize Agriturismo incorporation and IAP status. Begin wellness CAPEX.1  
* **Q4 2026:** Incorporate the Affiliate Service SRL. Draft service-level agreements between the entities for venue use.10  
* **April 2027:** Launch both entities. Overnight guests book via the Agriturismo; all weddings and day passes are processed through the SRL.1

This corporate structure ensures that Agriturismo Violiveto remains a compliant agricultural powerhouse while the Service SRL captures the full value of the luxury wellness and events market.
